Trade Hounds is built for the hardworking skilled tradespeople building our country and is America's largest professional app for the skilled trades. If you're in the trades, you should be a part of our community of 250,000+ proud craftspeople.

Here's what you can do:

- Showcase your great work with fellow tradespeople.
- Get tips from tradespeople to improve your craft.
- Discover cool projects across the country.
- Give advice to young tradespeople or those who need a hand.
- Need help on a project? Post your question or search for answers.
- Free hard hat stickers once you complete your work profile!
- Free rewards (like hi-vis t-shirts) for getting buddies to sign up!

We'd love to have you a part of our growing community. Are you one of us? (edited)
